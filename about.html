About-Us

about us

Nestled along the scenic Kedarnath Highway in the serene village of Gabani Gaun, Hotel Sachida Palace stands as a welcoming haven that beckons travelers to immerse themselves in the tranquility and natural splendor of the region. With its enchanting setting amidst the majestic landscapes of Uttarakhand, our establishment offers more than just accommodation – it promises an unforgettable experience that captures the essence of this picturesque destination.

As you step into the foyer of Hotel Sachida Palace, you'll instantly feel the warmth of our hospitality enveloping you. The lobby exudes an ambiance of rustic charm blended with modern comfort, setting the tone for your stay. The traditional architecture of the hotel seamlessly integrates with its surroundings, creating a harmonious blend of aesthetics that's both pleasing to the eye and soothing to the soul.

Our commitment to exceptional service is the cornerstone of your stay. The dedicated staff at Hotel Sachida Palace is a team of friendly faces, each personifying the spirit of true hospitality. From the moment you arrive, your comfort becomes our top priority. Whether it's a personalized welcome, assistance with your luggage, or guidance on local attractions and activities, our staff is always ready to go the extra mile.

Each room at Hotel Sachida Palace is thoughtfully designed to provide a cozy retreat after a day of exploration. The interiors are tastefully adorned with local artwork and textiles that reflect the rich cultural heritage of Uttarakhand. Comfortable furnishings, plush bedding, and modern amenities ensure that you have a restful stay amidst the breathtaking surroundings.

Our culinary offerings further enrich your experience. The on-site restaurant not only treats you to delectable local cuisine but also offers a fusion of flavors that cater to diverse palates. Imagine savoring a piping-hot bowl of traditional Garhwali dal overlooking the verdant valleys, or indulging in continental delicacies under the starlit sky – at Hotel Sachida Palace, every meal is a sensory journey.

or those seeking spiritual solace, our proximity to the revered Kedarnath Temple provides pilgrims with a convenient base. Whether you're embarking on a spiritual journey or embarking on adventurous treks through the lush trails, the hotel serves as a tranquil respite to rejuvenate both body and spirit.

At Hotel Sachida Palace, we believe that it's the finer details that transform a stay into a cherished memory. From arranging guided hikes to secret viewpoints, to curating personalized experiences like traditional village interactions, we strive to create moments that are as unique as our guests.

In essence, Hotel Sachida Palace isn't just a place to stay – it's a destination in itself. An embodiment of hospitality, comfort, and the unspoiled beauty of Uttarakhand, we invite you to join us in creating your own chapter of cherished moments amidst the allure of Gabani Gaun.

frequently asked questions

what are payment methods?

At Sachida Place, we offer flexible payment options to enhance your convenience. We accept payments through various online modes, including major credit and debit cards, as well as secure online payment platforms. Additionally, for those who prefer offline transactions, we also accept cash payments at our counter. Our aim is to ensure a smooth and hassle-free payment experience, catering to your preferred method, whether it's online or in the form of cash upon arrival.

Is there an age requirement for booking a room?

Sachida Place has an age requirement of 18 years for booking a room. This policy is in line with the legal age of adulthood in India. Guests must be at least 18 years old to make a reservation and stay at Sachida Place. This requirement helps ensure that guests are of legal age to enter into contractual agreements.

How can I make a reservation at Sachida Palace?

To secure your stay at Sachida Place, you have two easy options. Contact us directly through WhatsApp by sending your booking details, including check-in/out dates, guest count, room preferences, and any special requests. Our responsive team will guide you through the process swiftly. Alternatively, you can call our reservation line and speak with our knowledgeable staff who will assist you in reserving the perfect accommodations for your needs. We're excited to offer you a seamless and enjoyable booking experience at Sachida Place.

What is your check-in/check-out time?

Sachida Palace's check-in time is from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM, allowing guests to conveniently arrive and settle in during the day. For check-out, guests are kindly requested to complete the process by 9:00 AM in the morning. This timing ensures a smooth transition for departing guests and allows our dedicated staff to prepare the accommodations for incoming visitors. Your comfort and satisfaction during your stay are of utmost importance to us, and we appreciate your cooperation with our check-in and check-out timings.

Do your hotel room prices include meals, or are meals offered separately?

Sachida Palace offers flexibility when it comes to meals during your stay. While some of our room rates include meals, we also provide the option to choose meals separately based on your requirements. Our attentive staff is available to guide you through the various options and help you select the package that aligns best with your preferences. Feel free to reach out to us for more details on meal options and how they are structured in relation to our room prices.
