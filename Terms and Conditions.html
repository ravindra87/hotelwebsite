Terms and Conditions

1. Agreement

The contract between the guest and Sachida Palace and Homestay ("the Hotel") becomes effective upon receipt of written confirmation of the reservation, whether communicated via letter, fax, or email. The services provided are based on the description outlined in our brochure or on our official website.

2. Pricing

All prices are quoted in Indian Rupees (INR).

3. Payment Terms

Deposit: A deposit of 100% is required to secure the reservation. This deposit is equivalent to the total accommodation cost per person or as specified in the reservation.
If the reservation is not made through our website, the deposit must be settled within 15 days from the date of booking. A credit card number must be provided as a guarantee, even if the deposit has been paid.
In case payment is not received by the specified deadline, Sachida Palace and Homestay reserves the right to cancel the contract and impose a cancellation fee as per section 4.

Final Settlement.The remaining balance for the entire stay is to be settled in advance upon arrival.


4. Amendments and Cancellations

In the event of reservation cancellation, the following cancellation fees will apply:
Cancellation up to 30 days before the arrival date: No cancellation charge Cancellation from 29 to 15 days before the arrival date: Refund of up to 50% of the deposit Cancellation less than 15 days before the arrival date: No refund of the deposit (activities will also be charged if cancellation occurs less than three days before arrival) For written cancellations (email or post), the cancellation date is based on the date mentioned in the written cancellation document.
If you are unable to attend but transfer your booking to another individual, no additional costs will be incurred.
If the Hotel is unable to provide the booked accommodation due to its own fault, an equivalent compensation will be provided for the duration the room remains unavailable.
