Privacy Policy

At Sachida Palace and Homestay, we highly value your privacy and are committed to ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide to us during your reservation and stay.

Collection of Personal Information

During the reservation process and check-in, we may collect various types of personal information to enhance your experience and cater to your needs. This information is utilized for the purpose of customizing your stay, making necessary arrangements, and ensuring the security of our premises. The information we collect includes:
1. Your name, gender, personal and work contact details, business title.
2. Date and place of birth, nationality, and passport/visa information.
3. Guest stay details, such as arrival and departure dates, utilized services, special requests, room preferences, and observations related to your service preferences.
4. Telephone numbers dialed, received faxes, texts, and telephone messages.
5. Credit card details and account information.
6. Any information required to fulfill special requests, like health conditions necessitating specific accommodations or services.

Use of Information

We use the collected information to provide you with a tailored and enjoyable experience during your stay. This may involve:
1.Making adjustments based on your preferences.
2.Authorizing credit card transactions for additional days or services.
3.Responding to specific requests, such as accommodating health-related needs.

Data Security

We place significant emphasis on safeguarding your information. When you submit sensitive information through our website, appropriate measures are taken to ensure its security both online and offline.
Sensitive information, including credit card data, is encrypted and transmitted securely to us. Access to personally identifiable information is restricted to employees who require such access for specific job-related tasks, such as billing or customer service. Our computer systems and servers housing this information are maintained in a secure environment.
While we strive to protect your personal information, it's important to note that no data transmission over the internet is entirely secure. Hence, we cannot guarantee absolute security but assure you that we use the best industry practices to protect your information.

Key Replacement Process

In instances where a key is lost, our front desk agents will take precautions to ensure your security. This involves asking questions or requesting identification before providing a replacement key.
We appreciate your trust in Sachida Palace and Homestay, and we're committed to maintaining the confidentiality of your personal information. If you have any concerns or questions regarding our privacy practices, please don't hesitate to contact us.
Please note that this Privacy Policy may be subject to updates as needed, and we recommend reviewing it periodically for any changes.
